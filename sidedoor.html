SideDoor Inc.

Tech Stack: Vagrant, Gulp, HTML, CSS, LESS, Bootstrap, jQuery, CakePHP, Photoshop, Illustrator

SideDoor Inc. is a startup in Boulder, Colorado that works to help people buy and sell homes without real estate agents. I worked as a User Experience Designer and Frontend Developer. My job was to help with the redesign of SideDoor's web based platform, focusing on usability, functionality, and over all experience. I collaborated with a team which included, the CEO, the COO, and a backend developer. I focused primarily on the design and layout of pages as well as the frontend code. For design, I used Illustrator and Photoshop to create mockups for the desktop, tablet, and mobile versions of the site. I then sent those designs for review, tweaked what needed tweaking, and began coding. This was my first major project using Bootstrap, LESS, and CakePHP which was a great challenge for me. I loved becoming more familiar with these technologies and appreciate how they can be utilized to simplify code and save time. It was also a great opportunity and creative challenge to head up the design for this project.
